About Me

I am located in the "Silicon Forest" (Portland, Oregon) but originally from the Silicon Valley (San Jose, California). I moved here in 2005 and love it, but I do get my California fix during the holidays and the Summer time.

I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Telecommunication Management in 2001 but found it difficult to get a job at that time. The IT industry had "crashed" and I was now competing with seasoned vets. So, I became an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) and then became a Paramedic. I never lost my desire to get back into IT though, and now here I am!

I am a self-taught Front-End Web Developer with the drive and the ambition to learn and grow with the industry I grew up in.
